Earth Goddess Child

November 12, 2012 Â· 1 min Â· esthete

Earth goddess child, spirit of moonlight wild

Bless the earth with your footsteps, and more I beg

Bless the starlight with your eyes reflecting, your glittering lights

Hush the chaos with your soft-drumming heart

Earth goddess child, incarnation of moonlight wild

Heal an aching soul with your laughter singing, your murmuring magic

Holy and sacred and sky-clad you shimmer, and glimmer and shine

Goosebumps sprout in your winding wake

The moonlight will come to worship you in your purring sleep

The nightfall eagerly shrouds your form sublime

The stars will groan with envy

Surely such beauty must be a crime

Earth goddess child, blinding daughter of the sun

Let your shadow grace my worshipful eyes downcast

Let your breath mingle with mine as my prayers reach your ears

Gentle goddess I beg you accept this night my wind-borne kiss
