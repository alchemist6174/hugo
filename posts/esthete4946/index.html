Succubus Child

November 12, 2012 · 1 min · esthete

Under the green rushes your heart beats like a pagan drum

The shadows shape you by day

Watch evening fall like a darkening cloak with murmur and hum

Now comes the road and the way

Clad but in the night-sky your pale limbs leap and flash in flight

A call turns the wheels of your heart

Rushing to unspoken wishes you come a fleshy dream in the night

Aimed so true, an unerring dart

Fathered by a wish and mothered by a dream

You come

Eyes of water and skin of cream

You come

The veil of worlds is pulled aside

Your hand commands

Between hidden and seen, a rending tide

Your holy heart demands

Around the margin of sleep

where moonlight for beauty does weap, I hover…

‘Shhh’, you hasten…slender finger to lips

Sleeping unknowing my hands crawl to your hips

Drenched in shadow your form blends into mine

Spirit and flesh, a sacred rhyme

A kiss brings me falling home

And unguessing

… I wake alone
