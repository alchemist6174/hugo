To One Part of a Little Girl

Very short. Even if poetry is not your thing, give it a try - well, if pedo feelings are your thing!
March 14, 2013 · 1 min · englishperv

A part of her only, yet somehow the essence

just as strongly as her hairless little slit.

On the beach now I am in its presence,

and wanting it more than just a bit.

One black piece of flimsy swimwear

leaves two soft bubbles easily seen.

Only the crack itself is not bare,

and for the naked all I am keen.

This part, however, is piece-de-resistance

for many an adult beast like me.

By height and thinness she’s ten I sense,

too small anyway for sex I see.

I’m reflecting, though, on that rounded cleave

that it represents a way into her.

Some of a stiff penis I believe

could squeeze up there, happy, as it were.
