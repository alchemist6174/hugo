For those who wish to read this series in order, here is a glossary of each episode with a short summary of what is in each. As I posted each one, I’ve been calling them ‘stories’ as part of the ‘Pedo Ring Series’. I guess, more accurately they should be called ’episodes’. I’ll stick to that from now on, but each one could be read as an individual story if you wish - a kind of snapshot of pedo life - although making a bit more sense in the full context. You could dip in and out in any random order if you wish, but might be fun to see it through from the beginning.

As the series title suggests, this is a series of glimpses into the life and work of a fictional pedo ring, with a loose storyline linking them all. I have attempted realism in describing sexual activity between adults and children, and tried to give each character some credible personal development throughout. It is dark at times, for the sake of believability, but also has many sweet angles to it, reflecting the variety of men involved. Some of the male characters originated as actual readers of the story, and I wrote them into the script. It’s not too late for new applicants!
